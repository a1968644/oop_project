Welcome to Farm Game!

How to Play:
1. Click START to begin a new game.
2. Plant seeds and harvest crops to earn points.
3. Use the WASD keyboard to move between the grid.
4. Use mouse to click on the preferred seed 
and press spacebar to plant.
5. Wait the required growth time until the crops 
are ready and press H to harvest.
6. Continue earning points to unlock the next 
crop until all 3 have been unlocked.
7. Once all crops have been unlocked, planted and harvested, 
the player progresses to the next season.

Controls:
- Mouse for menus.
- ESC to pause or go back.
- Use WASD to navigate grid
